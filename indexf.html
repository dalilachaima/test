COMPUTER SCIENCE

Computer science is the study of algorithms, data structures, and computational processes that form the backbone of modern technology. It explores how information is processed, stored, and communicated through hardware and software systems. From developing applications and solving complex problems to advancing fields like artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, computer science plays a crucial role in shaping innovations that impact nearly every aspect of our daily lives.
